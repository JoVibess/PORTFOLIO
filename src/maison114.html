Maison 114

  • Category
  • Tools
  • Site
  • UX/UI design, Wordpress
  • maison114.ch

Maison 114 is a collaborative space located in Geneva, dedicated to hosting associations and public utility foundations. Acquired in 2022 by the Esse Quam Videri Foundation, the building was renovated and transformed in 2023-2024 to become a modern co-working and meeting space. My tasks were to design a mockup and create a WordPress site that reflect the values of friendliness, collaboration, and accessibility of Maison 114. To provide an intuitive user experience to promote its rental services and simplify contact.

1. Sitemap diagram

As a graphic charter already existed, the first step was to design a structure to define the general layout of the site.

2. Website design

Next, I created a mockup on Figma by integrating the provided elements. I edited the photos of the rooms in Lightroom to enhance their visual quality while optimizing their file size.

3. Wordpress

Once the mockup was approved, I was able to start building the site on WordPress using Elementor and custom CSS.

4. Launch

The final step was the launch of the site. I configured the connection to Google Search Console via DNS and created an XML sitemap to optimize the site's indexing and SEO.

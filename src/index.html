Who am I?

Passionate about design and web development, I am currently in my second year of a Bachelor's degree at MyDigitalSchool Annecy, specializing in web development. Alongside my studies, I am also training on the Three.js framework to create animations and 3D visualizations.

Born and raised in Mauritius for 17 years, my background and experiences have allowed me to develop a creative vision in my projects. Holding a degree in marine biology, this training gives me a unique approach to how I design and approach web development. My goal is to create solutions with an unconventional UX and UI design. Always motivated, I strive to take on new challenges and push the boundaries of digital creation.
